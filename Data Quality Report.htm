8.    Data Quality Issue : Continuous Variables : Skewness

 

Skewness

Characteristics

 

 

Strategy

Right Skew-Positive Skew

·       Right tail is longer

·       Many low values

·       Mean is usually greater than median

·       Most real world data is right skewed

Linear models assume normal distribution of predictors

Transformation

·       Square root

·       Log transform

·       Box-Cox

Left Skew-Negative Skew

·       Left tail is longer

·       Few low values

·       Mean is usually less than median
